## 5. Subject and Categories of Records Held
**Internal Records (Not automatically available):**
- Financial & Tax records
- Human Resources policies and employee data
- Intellectual Property, Source Code, and Trade Secrets
- Operational IT and Security logs

**Customer Data (Held as an Operator):**
- B2B tenant configurations, support tickets, conversation transcripts, and end-user data.
- **Note:** 6esk does not grant public PAIA requests for data belonging to our tenants. Requests for third-party personal information processed on behalf of our customers must be directed to the respective customer (the Responsible Party).

## 6. Request Procedure
To request access to a record, the requester must:
1. Complete the prescribed Form C (for private bodies) available from the Information Regulator.
2. Submit the form to the Information Officer via the email address provided above.
3. Pay the prescribed request fee (if applicable).
4. Provide sufficient detail to enable the Information Officer to identify the record and the requester.

## 7. Grounds for Refusal
6esk may refuse a request for information on grounds provided by PAIA, including but not limited to:
- Mandatory protection of privacy of a third party who is a natural person.
- Mandatory protection of commercial information of a third party (e.g., our SaaS customers).
- Mandatory protection of certain confidential information.
- Mandatory protection of records privileged from production in legal proceedings.
- Protection of 6esk's commercial activities (e.g., trade secrets, source code).
